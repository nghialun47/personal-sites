Privacy Policy

Last Updated: July 11, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to the professional video editing portfolio of Ethan Van ("we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is processed when you visit our website.

2. Third-Party Embeds & Services

Our website displays portfolio work using third-party embeds. When you view pages containing these embeds, your browser automatically establishes a direct connection to the third-party servers.

  • YouTube: We embed video players hosted on YouTube to showcase long-form editing work. We also query the Google YouTube Data API to display live, up-to-date video view counts. By interacting with these videos, YouTube/Google may collect technical identifiers, including your IP address, device specifications, and cookie data. You can review Google's Privacy Policy for details.
  • Instagram: We embed reels and posts hosted on Instagram to showcase short-form editing work. Meta Platforms, Inc. may collect data regarding your visit, device details, and play interactions. You can review Instagram's Privacy Policy for details.

3. Content Delivery & Security (Cloudflare)

We use Cloudflare, Inc. as our Content Delivery Network (CDN) and security provider to optimize page loading speeds, protect our infrastructure from Distributed Denial of Service (DDoS) attacks, and mitigate automated bot abuse.

In doing so, Cloudflare processes request metadata (including your IP address, request timestamp, browser configurations, and HTTP headers). Cloudflare may also set essential cookies (such as __cf_bm or security tokens) that are strictly necessary to verify client requests and maintain network security. These cookies do not harvest personal credentials or index browsing histories. For more information, please read the Cloudflare Privacy Policy.

4. Data Collection & Analytics

We do not directly collect, harvest, or sell personal identifiers or search behaviors on this site. We do not use proprietary analytical databases or registration portals.

Any statistical data displayed (e.g. view counts, video telemetry metrics) is derived either from public metrics or retrieved dynamically via official developer APIs representing video performance on external host sites.

5. Cookies & Local Storage

This website does not store proprietary tracking cookies. However, the third-party embeds (YouTube and Instagram) may set tracking cookies or write to local storage on your browser to measure playback stats, maintain quality standards, or deliver personalized recommendations. Additionally, Cloudflare may place essential cookies for security and bot-detection. You can manage or disable these cookies using your web browser's built-in privacy configurations.

6. External Links

Our website links to social media and professional networking sites, specifically LinkedIn. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or contents of these external sites.

7. Contact

If you have questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us via our LinkedIn profile page.
